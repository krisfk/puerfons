BRAND STORY

“Puerfons” stands for “fountain of youth” in Latin.

We created Purefons because we believe that a vigorous life is synchronized with both outer beauty and inner health. Enjoying the freedom and energy brought by a healthy body and mind, is the essence of youth.

Based on the latest life scientific developments, Puerfons products are designed to promote anti-aging functions that maintain our members’ beauty and health.

To delay the negative effects of the ageing process, we suggest a wide range of Puerfons’ products: to supplement ingredients that are missing from the intrinsic genes or lost from natural ageing process; to eliminate free radicals that scavenge the body and cause damage to cells, proteins and DNA; to reprogram dysfunctional cells to function actively as when you were young; to prevent physical and physiological decline by restoring lost functional ability of cells.

Youth is a state of mind. With Puerfons’ supplements and skincare products, the regenerated body will free you from the chain of physical limitation and support you with a rejuvenated vision and mindset. Puerfons is committed to turn progressive science into everyday life with our unique set of products and services; to transform anti-ageing technologies into everyone’s joyful daily routine.
